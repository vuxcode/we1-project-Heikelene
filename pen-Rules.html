Pen-Rules

1. Entrance into the bunny play-pen is only for guests over 5 years old.

Children between 5 to 10 years old must be accompanied
and supervised at all times by a grown-up.

2. Be calm and quiet

Bunnies get spooked and stressed by loud noises and sudden movements, so be mindful of that.

3. DO NOT pick up the bunnies whitout the permission from the staff!

4. Only pet the bunnies that approach you out of their own free will.

Please respect their individual boundaries

5. Pet the bunnies gently

Bunnies like to be petted around the face, ears and back.

6. Do not pull bunnies by their ears, tail, whiskers or any other body parts.

This hurt them, simple as that.

7. Only give them treats prepared from the staff.

Bunnies have sensitive tummies and cannot eat everything.
The treats that we provide are good for them.
