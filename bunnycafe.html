We're pleased to present to you the first launched bunny cafe in Sweden.
Our mission is to find loving lasting homes to abondoned pet rabbits but also to provide education about these wonderful
yet sometimes missunderstood critters.


The cafe is partnered with bunny rescue organizations and the staff here work with great passion,
to pave the way towards a brighter future for the 3rd most popular housepet; the pet rabbit.


At the cafe with its cozy interior you can sit, relax with something tasty from our menu and enjoy idle chitchat with a friend, or just exist.


There's a bunny playpen where you can pay extra to get to know the current residing bunnies at the cafe
by giving them some treats or just give them a gentle pat.


Our bunnies are up for adoption and if you are intrested you can sign up to a adoption.
We do also offer a 'bunny speed-dating' for those looking for a new friend for their bun.
If you're intrested in being a foster you can sign up for that as well, all help that we can get is welcome.
